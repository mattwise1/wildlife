About Uganda

Why Uganda is Africa's Best-Kept Secret

Nestled in the heart of East Africa, Uganda is home to half the world's remaining mountain gorillas, the mighty River Nile, and some of the continent's most diverse ecosystems — all within a country the size of the UK.

With 10 national parks, over 1,000 bird species, and warm hospitable people, Uganda offers an authentic, uncrowded safari experience that leaves visitors transformed.

Best Time to Visit Uganda

☀️

Dry Season

June – September & December – February

The best time for gorilla trekking and game drives. Trails are dry, animals gather around water sources, and visibility is excellent.

  • ✓ Easier gorilla trekking trails
  • ✓ Peak wildlife viewing at waterholes
  • ✓ Ideal for photography
🌧️

Green Season

March – May & October – November

Lush landscapes, fewer tourists, and lower prices. Bird watching is at its peak as migratory species arrive. Gorillas can still be trekked.

  • ✓ Lower prices — up to 30% off
  • ✓ Best for bird watching
  • ✓ Fewer crowds at parks
